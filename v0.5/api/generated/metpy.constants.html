constants¶

This is a collection of meteorologically significant constants. # noqa: E501.

Earth¶

Name Symbol Short Name Units Description
earth_avg_radius \(R_e\) Re \(\text{m}\) Avg. radius of the Earth
earth_gravity \(g\) g \(\text{m s}^{-2}\) Avg. gravity acceleration on Earth
earth_avg_angular_vel \(\Omega\) omega \(\text{rad s}^{-1}\) Avg. angular velocity of Earth
earth_sfc_avg_dist_sun \(d\) d \(\text{m}\) Avg. distance of the Earth from the Sun
earth_solar_irradiance \(S\) S \(\text{W m}^{-2}\) Avg. solar irradiance of Earth
earth_max_declination \(\delta\) delta \(\text{degrees}\) Max. solar declination angle of Earth
earth_orbit_eccentricity \(e\)   \(\text{None}\) Avg. eccentricity of Earth’s orbit

Water¶

Name Symbol Short Name Units Description
water_molecular_weight \(M_w\) Mw \(\text{g mol}^{-1}\) Molecular weight of water
water_gas_constant \(R_v\) Rv \(\text{J (K kg)}^{-1}\) Gas constant for water vapor
density_water \(\rho_l\) rho_l \(\text{kg m}^{-3}\) Nominal density of liquid water at 0C
wv_specific_heat_press \(C_{pv}\) Cp_v \(\text{J (K kg)}^{-1}\) Specific heat at constant pressure for water vapor
wv_specific_heat_vol \(C_{vv}\) Cv_v \(\text{J (K kg)}^{-1}\) Specific heat at constant volume for water vapor
water_specific_heat \(Cp_l\) Cp_l \(\text{J (K kg)}^{-1}\) Specific heat of liquid water at 0C
water_heat_vaporization \(L_v\) Lv \(\text{J kg}^{-1}\) Latent heat of vaporization for liquid water at 0C
water_heat_fusion \(L_f\) Lf \(\text{J kg}^{-1}\) Latent heat of fusion for liquid water at 0C
ice_specific_heat \(C_{pi}\) Cp_i \(\text{J (K kg)}^{-1}\) Specific heat of ice at 0C
density_ice \(\rho_i\) rho_i \(\text{kg m}^{-3}\) Density of ice at 0C

dry_air_spec_heat_press \(C_{pd}\) Cp_d \(\text{J (K kg)}^{-1}\) Specific heat at constant pressure for dry air
dry_air_spec_heat_vol \(C_{vd}\) Cv_d \(\text{J (K kg)}^{-1}\) Specific heat at constant volume for dry air
